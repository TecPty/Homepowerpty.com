# CRO and Conversion Checklist — Home Power PTY

## Value Proposition Clarity

- [ ] Wholesale positioning is visible above the fold on mobile without scrolling
- [ ] The headline communicates B2B value (pricing, bulk, distribution), not generic consumer messaging
- [ ] The subheadline or kicker reinforces the Panama / LatAm market angle
- [ ] There is no ambiguity about who the site is for (store owners, resellers, commercial buyers)

## Primary CTA (WhatsApp)

- [ ] At least one WhatsApp CTA is visible above the fold on mobile
- [ ] WhatsApp CTA uses action-oriented copy tied to business intent (e.g., "Consultar precios al por mayor", not "Contáctanos")
- [ ] CTA button contrast passes WCAG AA (4.5:1 minimum)
- [ ] Floating WhatsApp button does not overlap form inputs or product thumbnails on small screens
- [ ] WhatsApp open a pre-filled message with context (product or section)
- [ ] Multiple entry points exist: hero, product cards, contact section

## Product Catalog

- [ ] Filters are visible and predictable on mobile without instruction
- [ ] Filter state is clearly communicated (selected vs. unselected)
- [ ] Empty state is handled if a filter combination returns no products
- [ ] Product card shows: name, thumbnail, category — enough to evaluate without drilling in
- [ ] Tapping a product card leads to a logical next step (detail, WhatsApp CTA, or inquiry)
- [ ] Product detail pages (productos/) have standalone WhatsApp or inquiry CTAs

## Trust Signals

- [ ] Client logos are visible and legible on mobile (not cropped or overflow)
- [ ] Testimonials contain specific, credible details (name/business, not generic praise)
- [ ] About section communicates years of experience, volume, or distribution reach
- [ ] Contact information is findable without scrolling to the footer

## Lead Forms

- [ ] Wholesale form fields match what a B2B buyer actually needs to submit
- [ ] Form labels clearly identify which form is for wholesale vs. careers
- [ ] Required vs. optional fields are visually distinguished
- [ ] Success and error states give specific, actionable feedback (not just "Error")
- [ ] Forms are reachable via the main navigation or a visible scroll CTA

## Friction Audit

- [ ] Number of clicks/taps from landing to WhatsApp open is ≤ 3
- [ ] No modals interrupt the primary conversion path on first visit
- [ ] Scroll depth required to reach primary CTA is ≤ 2 viewport heights on mobile
- [ ] No content shifts or layout jank that interrupts reading flow
